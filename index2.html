langage

Admission to the University of Oxford is to offer the Cambridge Certificate of English Proficiency at the highest level of 191 and 185 for each section. Or obtain a standard average of 185 as a minimum, and 176 in the rest of the language skills. Submit the Cambridge Advanced Certificate (CAE) and obtain the highest average score of 191, in addition to obtaining a score of 185 in the remaining sections, or obtaining a standard level of 185, 176 in the remaining language skills.

Study fees

Undergraduate tuition fees are £9,250 per year for UK and EU students, and £24,750 per year for international students. Add the cost of living, which is estimated at between £12,000 and £19,000 per year, and you're looking at a very expensive education. Tuition fees for international students exceed £30,000 per year
